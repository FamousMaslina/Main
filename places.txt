New York City:

The most populous city in the United States.
Located in the state of New York.
Home to many iconic landmarks, such as the Empire State Building and the Statue of Liberty.

London:

The capital of the United Kingdom.
Located on the River Thames.
Home to many historical sites, such as the Tower of London and Buckingham Palace.

Paris:

The capital of France.
Located on the Seine River.
Known for its romantic atmosphere and its many cultural attractions, such as the Eiffel Tower and the Louvre Museum.

Tokyo:

The capital of Japan.
Located on Honshu Island.
One of the most populous cities in the world.
Known for its vibrant culture and its many modern attractions, such as the Tokyo Skytree and the Akihabara electronics district.

Sydney:

The largest city in Australia.
Located on the east coast of the country.
Home to many famous landmarks, such as the Sydney Opera House and the Harbour Bridge.
